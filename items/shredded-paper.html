Can I Compost Shredded paper?

Yes

Shredded paper can be composted.

What to Know

  • Shredded paper is a great 'brown' or carbon-rich material for composting
  • It helps balance the 'green' nitrogen-rich materials and improves air circulation in the compost pile
  • Use only plain paper without glossy finishes, colored inks, or chemical treatments

Tips

  • Wet the shredded paper before adding to the compost pile to prevent it from blowing away
  • Mix it well with 'green' materials like food scraps
  • Avoid using too much paper at once, as it can mat together and slow down decomposition
  • Shred paper into small pieces for faster breakdown
