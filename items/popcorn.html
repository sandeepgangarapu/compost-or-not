Can I Compost Popcorn?

Yes

Popcorn can be composted.

What to Know

  • Popcorn, both popped and unpopped kernels, can be composted
  • It's considered a 'green' material, adding nitrogen to your compost
  • Popped popcorn will break down faster than unpopped kernels

Tips

  • Avoid adding buttered or heavily salted popcorn as excessive oil and salt can harm your compost
  • If adding a large amount, mix it well with other materials to prevent clumping
  • Crush popped popcorn into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
