Can I Compost Shampoo?

No

Shampoo cannot be composted.

What to Know

  • Most shampoos contain chemicals, fragrances, and preservatives that can be harmful to the composting process and the environment
  • These ingredients may disrupt the balance of beneficial microorganisms in your compost pile

Tips

  • Instead of composting, consider using eco-friendly, biodegradable shampoos or looking for ways to recycle empty shampoo bottles
  • For a more sustainable approach, try shampoo bars that come with minimal or recyclable packaging
