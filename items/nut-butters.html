Can I Compost Nut butters?

Yes

Nut butters can be composted.

What to Know

  • Nut butters are compostable but should be added in moderation
  • They are high in fats and oils, which can slow down decomposition and potentially attract pests if used excessively

Tips

  • Add nut butters in small amounts and mix well with other compost materials
  • Spread thinly over dry, carbon-rich materials like leaves or shredded paper
  • Avoid adding large globs, as they can create anaerobic pockets in your compost pile
