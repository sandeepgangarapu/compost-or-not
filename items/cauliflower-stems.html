Can I Compost Cauliflower stems?

Yes

Cauliflower stems can be composted.

What to Know

  • Cauliflower stems are safe to compost and provide valuable nutrients to your compost pile
  • They are considered green materials and can be a good source of nitrogen
  • However, they may take longer to break down compared to softer vegetable scraps

Tips

  • Chop the stems into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Mix with brown materials like dry leaves or cardboard to maintain a good carbon-to-nitrogen ratio
  • If you notice any strong odors, add more brown materials and turn the pile to improve aeration
