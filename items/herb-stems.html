Can I Compost Herb stems?

Yes

Herb stems can be composted.

What to Know

  • Herb stems are excellent for composting
  • They're rich in nitrogen and break down relatively quickly
  • Both fresh and dried herb stems can be added to your compost pile

Tips

  • Chop longer stems into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Mix with brown materials for balanced compost
  • Avoid adding stems from herbs treated with pesticides or chemical fertilizers
