Can I Compost Chips?

Yes

Chips can be composted.

What to Know

  • Chips can be composted, but they're not ideal
  • They're often high in salt and oils, which can be detrimental to your compost pile if added in large quantities
  • They also break down slowly due to their processed nature

Tips

  • Use chips sparingly in your compost
  • Crush them into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Mix thoroughly with other compost materials to dilute the salt and oil content
  • Avoid adding flavored or cheese-coated chips as they may attract pests
