Can I Compost Pet hair?

Yes

Pet hair can be composted.

What to Know

  • Pet hair is a nitrogen-rich 'green' material that can be added to compost
  • It breaks down relatively quickly and adds nutrients to the soil
  • However, use it in moderation to maintain balance in your compost pile

Tips

  • Scatter pet hair thinly throughout the compost pile rather than adding it in clumps
  • Mix it well with 'brown' materials like dry leaves or shredded paper
  • Avoid using hair from pets treated with flea medications or other chemicals
  • If possible, wash and rinse the hair before composting to remove any residues
