Can I Compost Nut shells?

Yes

Nut shells can be composted.

What to Know

  • Nut shells can be composted, but they break down very slowly
  • They're considered a 'brown' material, adding carbon to your compost
  • Some nut shells, like walnuts, contain juglone which can inhibit plant growth

Tips

  • Crush or grind nut shells into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Use in moderation and mix well with other compost materials
  • Avoid using black walnut shells in compost intended for sensitive plants
