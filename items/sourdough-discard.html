Can I Compost Sourdough discard?

Yes

Sourdough discard can be composted.

What to Know

  • Sourdough discard is a great addition to compost bins
  • It's rich in nutrients and microorganisms that can help accelerate the composting process
  • However, it's considered a 'green' material, so it should be balanced with 'brown' materials

Tips

  • Mix sourdough discard thoroughly with other compost materials to prevent clumping
  • Use in moderation to maintain proper carbon-to-nitrogen ratio
  • If you notice any odors, add more brown materials like dried leaves or cardboard to balance it out
