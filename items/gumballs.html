Can I Compost Gumballs?

No

Gumballs cannot be composted.

What to Know

  • Gumballs are not suitable for composting
  • They are made from synthetic materials like polyethylene and polyvinyl acetate, which do not break down naturally and can introduce harmful chemicals into your compost

Tips

  • Instead of composting, dispose of gumballs in the regular trash
  • If you're looking to reduce waste, consider switching to natural chewing gums made from chicle or other biodegradable materials
