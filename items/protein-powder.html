Can I Compost Protein powder?

No

Protein powder cannot be composted.

What to Know

  • Protein powder is not recommended for composting
  • It's a processed food product that can attract pests, create odors, and potentially disrupt the balance of your compost pile

Tips

  • Instead of composting, consider using leftover protein powder in recipes like smoothies or baked goods
  • If you must dispose of it, mix small amounts into your regular trash or check with local waste management for proper disposal methods
