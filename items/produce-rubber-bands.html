Can I Compost Produce rubber bands?

No

Produce rubber bands cannot be composted.

What to Know

  • Produce rubber bands are typically made from synthetic materials that do not break down in compost
  • They can persist in the environment for many years and may contaminate your compost with microplastics

Tips

  • Instead of composting, reuse rubber bands for organizing or crafts
  • If disposal is necessary, check with your local recycling facility for proper disposal methods
  • Consider using compostable produce ties or twine made from natural fibers as alternatives
