Can I Compost Cake?

Yes

Cake can be composted.

What to Know

  • Cake can be composted, but it's important to do so in moderation
  • It's considered a 'green' material due to its high nitrogen content
  • Cake can attract pests if not managed properly in your compost pile

Tips

  • Break the cake into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Mix thoroughly with 'brown' materials like dried leaves or cardboard to balance the carbon-to-nitrogen ratio
  • Avoid composting cakes with excessive frosting or those containing dairy products, as these can cause odor issues and attract pests
