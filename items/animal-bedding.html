Can I Compost Animal bedding?

Yes

Animal bedding can be composted.

What to Know

  • Animal bedding from herbivores (e
  • g
  • , rabbits, guinea pigs, hamsters) is excellent for composting
  • It's rich in nitrogen and often already mixed with carbon-rich materials like wood shavings or straw
  • Avoid bedding from carnivores or omnivores like cats and dogs

Tips

  • Ensure the bedding is from healthy animals
  • Mix thoroughly with other compost materials to prevent clumping
  • If using wood shavings, be aware they may take longer to break down
  • Consider pre-composting in a separate bin before adding to your main compost pile to reduce any odor issues
