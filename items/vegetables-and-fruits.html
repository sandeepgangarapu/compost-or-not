Can I Compost Vegetables and Fruits?

Yes

Vegetables and fruits are excellent for composting.

What to Know

  • Most vegetable and fruit scraps are great for composting.
  • They provide essential nutrients to your compost.
  • Chop larger pieces to speed up decomposition.

Tips

  • Mix with "brown" materials like dried leaves for balanced compost.
  • Avoid adding diseased plants to your compost.
  • Citrus peels are okay but use in moderation as they can be acidic.
