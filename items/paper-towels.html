Can I Compost Paper towels?

Yes

Paper towels can be composted.

What to Know

  • Paper towels are generally compostable, but only if they're unbleached and haven't been used with harsh chemicals
  • They're considered a 'brown' material, providing carbon to your compost pile

Tips

  • Tear paper towels into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Avoid composting paper towels used with cleaning products or to wipe up oils
  • If possible, use paper towels made from recycled materials for better environmental impact
