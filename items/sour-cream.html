Can I Compost Sour cream?

Yes

Sour cream can be composted.

What to Know

  • Sour cream is a dairy product that can be composted, but it should be used in moderation
  • It's considered a 'green' material, high in nitrogen
  • Be aware that it may attract pests and create odors if not managed properly

Tips

  • Add sour cream in small amounts and mix it well with brown materials like leaves or sawdust
  • Bury it in the center of your compost pile to deter pests
  • If using a closed bin, ensure good aeration to prevent anaerobic conditions and bad smells
