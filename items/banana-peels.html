Can I Compost Banana peels?

Yes

Banana peels can be composted.

What to Know

  • Banana peels are excellent for composting
  • They're rich in potassium and other nutrients that benefit your compost and garden
  • They break down relatively quickly but can be slimy if added in large quantities

Tips

  • Cut banana peels into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Mix them with brown materials like dry leaves or cardboard to balance moisture and prevent odors
  • If using a lot of peels, consider freezing them and adding them to the compost gradually to avoid attracting pests
