Can I Compost Hair?

Yes

Hair can be composted.

What to Know

  • Hair is rich in nitrogen and can be a good addition to compost
  • It breaks down slowly but adds valuable nutrients
  • Both human and pet hair can be composted

Tips

  • Spread hair thinly throughout the compost pile to prevent clumping
  • Mix with carbon-rich materials like leaves or paper
  • Avoid using hair treated with chemical dyes or other products as these may introduce unwanted substances to your compost
