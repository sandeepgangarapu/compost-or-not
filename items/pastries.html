Can I Compost Pastries?

Yes

Pastries can be composted.

What to Know

  • Pastries are compostable but should be added in moderation
  • They are considered 'green' materials and can attract pests if not managed properly
  • Avoid pastries with dairy fillings or excessive fats

Tips

  • Break pastries into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Mix thoroughly with 'brown' materials like dry leaves or paper to balance carbon-to-nitrogen ratio
  • If possible, bury pastries in the center of your compost pile to deter pests
