Can I Compost Apple cores?

Yes

Apple cores can be composted.

What to Know

  • Apple cores are excellent for composting
  • They're rich in nitrogen and break down relatively quickly
  • The seeds contain small amounts of cyanide, but this isn't harmful to your compost or garden

Tips

  • Chop apple cores into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Mix with brown materials like dry leaves or paper for balanced compost
  • If you notice fruit flies, bury the apple cores deeper in the compost pile or cover with a layer of brown materials
