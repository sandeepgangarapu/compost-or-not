Can I Compost Bread?

Yes

Bread can be composted.

What to Know

  • Bread can be composted, but it should be added in moderation
  • It's a 'green' material that adds nitrogen to your compost
  • However, bread can attract pests and rodents if not properly managed in your compost pile

Tips

  • Break bread into small pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Bury it in the center of your compost pile to deter pests
  • Mix with plenty of 'brown' materials like dry leaves or paper to balance moisture and prevent clumping
