Can I Compost Shrimp shells?

Yes

Shrimp shells can be composted.

What to Know

  • Shrimp shells are compostable and rich in nitrogen and calcium
  • They break down slowly and can attract pests if not managed properly
  • The shells may create a slight odor during decomposition

Tips

  • Crush the shells into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Bury them deep in the compost pile to deter pests
  • Balance with carbon-rich materials like leaves or paper
  • Avoid adding large quantities at once to prevent odor issues
