Can I Compost Cooked vegetables?

Yes

Cooked vegetables can be composted.

What to Know

  • Cooked vegetables can be composted, but they may attract pests if not managed properly
  • They break down quickly and add nutrients to your compost
  • Avoid composting vegetables cooked with oils, fats, or animal products

Tips

  • Bury cooked vegetables in the center of your compost pile to deter pests
  • Mix with plenty of brown materials like leaves or cardboard to balance moisture and prevent odors
  • If possible, let cooked vegetables cool completely before adding to the compost
