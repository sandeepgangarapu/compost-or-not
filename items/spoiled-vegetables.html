Can I Compost Spoiled vegetables?

Yes

Spoiled vegetables can be composted.

What to Know

  • Spoiled vegetables are excellent for composting
  • They break down quickly and provide valuable nutrients to your compost
  • However, they may attract pests if not managed properly

Tips

  • Bury spoiled vegetables in the center of your compost pile to deter pests
  • Chop larger pieces for faster decomposition
  • Balance with brown materials like dry leaves or newspaper to manage moisture and odor
