Can I Compost Meat and Dairy?

Yes, with caution

Meat and dairy can be composted, but require special care.

What to Know

  • Meat and dairy can attract pests if not managed properly.
  • These items may produce strong odors during decomposition.
  • Requires higher temperatures to break down safely.

Tips

  • Use a Bokashi bucket or similar sealed composting system.
  • Add only small amounts relative to your total compost volume.
  • Ensure your compost reaches high temperatures (above 140°F/60°C).
  • Bury meat and dairy scraps deep in the center of your pile.
  • Consider avoiding these items if you're new to composting.
