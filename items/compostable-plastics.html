Can I Compost Compostable plastics?

No

Compostable plastics cannot be composted.

What to Know

  • While labeled as 'compostable', these plastics typically require industrial composting facilities with specific conditions to break down properly
  • They do not decompose effectively in backyard compost bins

Tips

  • Instead of composting, look for local industrial composting facilities that accept these materials
  • Alternatively, reduce use of compostable plastics and opt for reusable items or truly biodegradable materials like paper or bamboo products
