Can I Compost Cotton balls?

Yes

Cotton balls can be composted.

What to Know

  • Cotton balls are compostable as they are made from natural fibers
  • However, ensure they are 100% cotton and not synthetic blends
  • They decompose slowly compared to food scraps

Tips

  • Tear or shred the cotton balls before adding to increase surface area for faster decomposition
  • Use in moderation as they are considered a 'brown' material
  • Avoid cotton balls with chemicals, such as those used for removing nail polish
