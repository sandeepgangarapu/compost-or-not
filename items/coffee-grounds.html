Can I Compost Coffee Grounds?

Yes

Coffee grounds are excellent for composting.

What to Know

  • Coffee grounds are rich in nitrogen, essential for composting.
  • They help improve soil structure.
  • Can help balance the carbon-to-nitrogen ratio in your compost.

Tips

  • Both used and unused coffee grounds can be composted.
  • Paper coffee filters can also be composted along with the grounds.
  • Mix coffee grounds with other compost materials for best results.
