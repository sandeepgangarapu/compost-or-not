Can I Compost Potato peels?

Yes

Potato peels can be composted.

What to Know

  • Potato peels are excellent for composting
  • They break down easily and add valuable nutrients to your compost
  • However, avoid composting potato peels if they're green or have sprouts, as these contain solanine which can be harmful

Tips

  • Chop potato peels into smaller pieces to speed up decomposition
  • Mix with brown materials like dry leaves or cardboard for balanced compost
  • If you're concerned about potato plants sprouting in your compost, consider boiling the peels before adding them to kill any remaining eyes
