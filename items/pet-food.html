Can I Compost Pet food?

Yes

Pet food can be composted.

What to Know

  • Pet food can be composted, but it should be done with caution
  • It may attract pests and animals to your compost pile
  • Only compost dry pet food, not wet food or treats with preservatives

Tips

  • Bury pet food deep in the compost pile to deter pests
  • Use in moderation as part of a balanced compost mix
  • Avoid composting pet food if you have issues with rodents or other wildlife in your area
