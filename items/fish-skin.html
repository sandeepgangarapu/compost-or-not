Can I Compost Fish skin?

Yes

Fish skin can be composted.

What to Know

  • Fish skin is compostable but requires careful management
  • It's rich in nitrogen and can attract pests if not handled properly
  • The strong odor can be a concern for some gardeners

Tips

  • Bury fish skin deep in the center of your compost pile to mask odors
  • Mix with plenty of carbon-rich materials like leaves or sawdust
  • Use sparingly to avoid overwhelming your compost with protein and fats
  • Consider using a closed composting system to deter animals
